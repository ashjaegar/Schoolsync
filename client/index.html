About Jindal Adarsh School

Founded in 1995, Jindal Adarsh School (JAS) stands as a premier educational institution dedicated to excellence in New Delhi. Under the visionary guidance of our founders, the school provides an enriching environment where academic rigor meets character building.

Our curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning. With state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated educators, and comprehensive co-curricular programs, we ensure that every student is equipped with the skills necessitated by a rapidly modernizing world while remaining rooted in strong ethical values.
